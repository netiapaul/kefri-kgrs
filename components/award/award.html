The Research grant Award

The Research Grant Award covers cost of:

Research and academic resources as defined by the approved study requirements.

The Research Grant Award WILL NOT cover:

Tuition fees.

Living expenses.

Book allowance. However any text books or software procured will remain KEFRI property.
